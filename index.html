At Kinera, we offer a wide selection of high-quality, eco-friendly household cleaning tools, each crafted with care to meet the diverse needs of our customers. Our main products, including natural brooms and cleaning brushes, are designed to make household cleaning easier, more effective, and environmentally responsible. Here’s an overview of our featured products :
  1. Sorghum/Millet Broom – A natural broom made from sorghum fibers, durable and effective for sweeping dust and dirt off floors.
  2. Arenga Broom – Crafted from the fibers of the aren tree, this broom is strong and ideal for both indoor and outdoor use.
  3. Glagah/Arenga Broom – Made from premium materials, this broom can handle various types of dirt and is weather-resistant.
  4. Coconut Fiber Hand Brush – A versatile brush made from coconut fibers, suitable for cleaning various surfaces without causing damage.
  5. Fiber Rope – A strong and versatile fiber rope, suitable for various uses, from household tasks to farming applications.
